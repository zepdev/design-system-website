# Colors

Brand colors are the basis to maintain consistency and accessibility throughout Zeppelin. The color range is dominated by the three primary colors, Indigo Blue, Rental Red and Caterpillar Yellow. A palette of grays serve as accents and are used for emotional effect. This overview can help you to create a color theme that reflects a strategic business unit or product.
