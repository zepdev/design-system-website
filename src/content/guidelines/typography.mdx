# Typography

As of Version 1.0.0, ZDS now uses only Roboto fonts; **Roboto (light: 300, regular: 400, medium: 500, and bold: 700)** and **Roboto Condensed (regular: 400, bold: 700)**. There are three typos of font styles; **Normal, Bold, and Display**.

All typography components have a black ('positive') and a white verison ('negative'). Black will be the default, the use of white needs a modifier to the css name **(-negative)**.
