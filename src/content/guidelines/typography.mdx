# Typography

As of Version 1.0.0, ZDS now uses only Roboto fonts (light: 300, regular: 400, medium: 500, and bold: 700). Headings are comprised of Normal and Bold versions. All typography components have a black (default) and a white verison (-inverse) that should be used against black or colored backgrounds.
