# Typography

The Zeppelin Group corporate fonts for digital products are Roboto and Teko. Tungsten is a flat-sided sans-serif typeface. It allows a distinction to the original brand and conveys self-confidence, modernity and openness. Both fonts are web-safe and optimized for digital media.
